Opportunities should not only be in my domain of work, but very diversified. Example, I am a lecturer at a univeristy, but nothing stops me from buying a boat to fish, or a bus to run taxi, etc. 



There should also be opportuities to further education certificate, associates, degree, masters, etc, with all their varying costs which should unlock more opportunities. 

There should be a market. 

You should be able to enter side hustles, like selling juice on the side of the road. But those opportunities with low barriers to entry will be saturated. 

Players should be able to partner up to create a business, sharing debts and income. 

The more money you have, more opportuinties should be unlocked. 

Also there should be an option to crowd fund, asking friends for money, and some will demand either a share in the profit or varying interest rates. 


Opportunities should be randomized for all players in the ecosystem.
